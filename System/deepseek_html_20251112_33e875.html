Tiered Membership Structure

FSRPWL operates on a clear two-tier system with escalating standards for participation.

Minimum Age: 13

Tier 1: Civilian

The foundational layer of our city, playing regular citizens, business owners, and civilians.

  • Must demonstrate "very high level of knowledge" and maturity
  • Applicants who sound underage will be denied
  • Foundation for all roleplay scenarios
  • Access to civilian vehicles and properties
Minimum Age: 14

Tier 2: Department Member

The elite standard with requirements "three times higher" than civilian applications.

  • More complex applications and in-depth knowledge tests
  • Practical roleplay assessments
  • Access to specialized department equipment
  • Higher expectations for activity and professionalism

The FSRPWL Advantage

What truly sets our community apart is our technological infrastructure and guiding philosophy.

Guided Experience Philosophy

FSRPWL is not a chaotic free-for-all. It is a carefully managed environment where members are expected to have fun within a structured framework of realism and order.

Our strict rules, whitelist process, and advanced systems all work in concert to create a specific, high-quality roleplay experience for all members.

Advanced Custom Systems

The server is powered by proprietary software developed in-house by our owner, creating a seamless and immersive experience that far surpasses standard public bots.

Our custom 911 CAD System integrates Discord with Roblox for realistic call-taking, unit dispatch, and incident management.
